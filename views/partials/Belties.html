About Belted Galloways

According to the Belted Galloway Society, Belties are an ancient breed developed centuries ago in Galloway Scotland. They are famous for their belted white stripe surrounded by black, red or dun.* Belties have been shown to provide more protein with just a fraction of fat calories and cholesterol. #

* https://beltie.org/about.php

# https://beltie.org//pdf/about/Beltie-Beef.pdf
